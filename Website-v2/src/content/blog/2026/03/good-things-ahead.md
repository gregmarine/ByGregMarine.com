Some days you wake up and just feel it — that quiet sense that something good is coming. Not because everything is perfect, but because you've learned to hold things a little more loosely than you used to.

I don't have a grand point to make today. Just wanted to write something small and true: the small rituals matter. The morning coffee. The walk. The chapter before bed. These are the things that hold a week together.

Here's to whatever comes next.
