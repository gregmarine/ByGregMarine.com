I started working on a note taking app yesterday. I'm currently calling it NoteSprout. It is inspired by the handwritten notes and journaling I do everyday. There are things I like and don't like about all the e-ink note apps on my tablets. I've thought about creating my own for months now. Well, I finally started the process. So far, I've fleshed out the concept and started working out the data-model. My next step is to create the minimal viable product for the proof of concept. Basically, a notebook with pages and the ability to write on the pages and erase parts of what is written. All saving to a file that I can then reopen later. Basic starter, "it's just paper" sort of app. And then *grow* from there...hehehe
