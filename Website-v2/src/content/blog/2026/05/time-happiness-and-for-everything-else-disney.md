Something on my mind is how most of us think we have more time. More time to live our lives. More time with the ones we love. Sure, a lot of people say they don't have enough time to this, that, and the other. But I think deep down, we think that we have more time to do all of those things we say we don't have time for "today." To which I say, we have time for all of the things we want to have time for. Meaning, we typically are doing the things we want to do. Or spending time with those we actually do want to spend time with. Otherwise, why would be spending time doing what we are doing and who we are doing it with?
