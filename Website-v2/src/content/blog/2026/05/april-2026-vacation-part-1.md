It might be hard to imagine having 3 major vacations in 8 months. But we did it! I wrote all about our first one that we took in August last year. I didn't quite finish writing about our November one...LOL But I'm back now to start documenting our 3rd one. Last month, we another amazing adventure! Interestingly, we have our 4th just ahead of us in 2 weeks...LOL That's right! 4 in 9 months. It's so exciting 🤩

I've already written a little bit about my travels going to California and coming home. So, I'll not talk any more about that. In this entry, I'll focus on our first day together. As always, I traveled the day before and stayed in the hotel that night. So naturally, my day started off having the Ayres breakfast. It was delicious as always 😋 I don't always have apple juice when I travel. And I never do at home. But I do when I'm at Ayres with Cherie. Although she wasn't there yet, I still had apple juice that first morning. It's tradition!

I wrote one of my "Morning, Afternoon, Evening" entries that day. So part of my morning was spent writing. I also steamed my clothes to get them ready for our Disney adventures to come. It was a relaxing way to start the day. Around lunchtime, I went to the grocery. I got some things from Sprouts and Walmart to have at the hotel and throughout the visit as a whole. Buying water at Ayres is expensive. I was able to get a 12 pack of bottles at Walmart for less than 1 bottle at the hotel...LOL Plus I got snacks for between meals. I can always bring my own snacks. But I can't bring water. They don't allow that on the plane. So, from now on, I plan to make a grocery trip on these vacations.

When I got back to the hotel, I did the afternoon part of that day's entry. And then I headed over to ARTIC to await Cherie's arrival. Her train was running a little late by about 11 minutes. Apparently one of those railroad crossing arms was down and in the middle of the track at one of the crossroads. This time, she was able to notice the car number of the train car she road. That made it really easy to know which set of doors I'd see her come out of. As soon as I saw her, I was elated and quite literally leaped with joy...LOL I did a little Trex trot over to Trexina...hehehe

As we were walking through ARTIC, we exchanged gifts. Hers for me was for my birthday. I gave her a few little things like some new pins and I had written her a letter. I also included some Trex things; as did she include some Trexina things for me. She made me some chicken and rice like she did back in November. That is another one of our traditions now 🤩 Once she was all checked in, we parted ways to get ready for our Dapper Day Eve! This was when I had the chicken and rice. It was amazing! I enjoyed the texture of the rice more this time. She told me she had made it *her* way this time...hehehe It was excellent!
