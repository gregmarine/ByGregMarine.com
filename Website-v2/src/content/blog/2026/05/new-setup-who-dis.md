Now that I'm able to log into my work's virtual desktop from my MacBook, I've made a positive adjust to my workspace. I moved my work laptop off my main desk and move my MacBook in 🤩 I bought an Apple Magic Mouse for my MacBook because I now have the MacBook up on a stand. I'm using my Qwerkywriter keyboard with it and the large external monitor. It's a great setup and it allows me to work from a single desk when I'm doing things that require the *power* of a laptop; such as work or doing some of personal projects.

Also, now that I'm back to using my MacBook for blogging, and generally working with my new website, I get the luxury of using this fancy keyboard...hehehe I hadn't used it with my MacBook yet...LOL And the reason I bought it in the first place was to write. I originally got it to use with my Supernote Manta. I then started using it for work. But my poor MacBook looked with a side eye in disappointment that it couldn't also use the fancy keyboard 👀 LOL j/k
