Today is one of the most important days in my life. I'm one who remembers important anniversaries and significant events from my life. Today is one such day. And I'm especially grateful for this one. On top of what day it is, this year's anniversary comes on the heals of our latest adventure 🤩

Cherie and I originally became Twitter mutual friends on March 10, 2022. I remember that day as well. It is actually the day that led to today being what it is...hehehe Although she had made a post that got the attention of DisTwitter, what led to us eventually speaking directly was a post I made about doing Disney character paintings. Her response to that post inspired "Bambi's Bird", the first in a traditional painting I make for her each year.

About a year later, I created "Bambi's Bird" and sent it to her. I included a note with my phone number to let her know I was just a phone call or text away if she ever needed. And then on May 20th that year, she did reach out. But she was reaching out because she knew I was the one who needed a friend 🥹 I appreciated her so much for reaching out to me that day 3 years ago. Had she not posted her "storybook" post, hadn't responded to my "painting" post, and not sent me that text 3 years, my life would be quite different today. And I know hers would be quite different as well.
