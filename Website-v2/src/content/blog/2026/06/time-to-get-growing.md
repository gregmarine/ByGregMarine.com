I'm sure I mentioned how rough Winter was for me. But Spring was so amazing 🤩 The contrast between the two is inspiring. There are some lessons to be learned from both experiences. Now that June is here, and I don't have any immediate travel plans, I'm glad to be able to live in a retrospective and introspective state of mind for a few weeks and see how I can learn from those lessons and experiences.

I'll not rehash all that happened throughout both seasons. Today is more of a starting point to simply say that I'm excited about how all of this impacts my life. I had looked ahead to June months before and intentionally left it plan-less so I could relax a bit. But relaxing isn't what I need for an entire month. What I've decided I want to do is a lot of thinking...LOL And some doing as I find ways of applying what I've learned about God, myself, and life in general, so far this year.

This will be a great time to work on my routine as well. I seem to always be working on that...LOL But these last 6 or so months have been routine-chaotic at best. The moment I "settled" into something that resembled a routine, something interrupted it. There isn't anything wrong with that, really. It's just what has been happening for months now. I'm hoping in June to work on some changes that will make a positive impact on what I am able to do for routines.
