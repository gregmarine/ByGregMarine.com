When I moved to Florida, Mom and Dad sent me a little "Welcome Home" care package. One of the items was a photo "frame" that says "Relax" on it and it holds a photograph with a little clip at the top. I love it and it has been on display for years no. I decided to hang it in my bathroom...hehehe I've had guests comment how much they love my bathroom decor 🤩 This is the first bathroom I've ever had that actually enjoy being in...LOL The "Relax" photo is one of the reasons why I enjoy my bathroom now. I imagine I'll hang this in every bathroom I have from now on...hehehe

![View of the Gulf from Brasher Park in Port Richey, Florida](https://res.cloudinary.com/dh6izdwyd/image/upload/v1781037182/IMG_9721_ph3wqc.jpg "View of the Gulf from Brasher Park in Port Richey, Florida")

The first photo I displayed on this was from one of the first places I explored in my new home. I took this photo on August 4th, 2022 at Brasher Park in Port Rickey, Florida. Back then, I would frequently visit this park to enjoy the views of the Gulf. There were even many times I would kayak from the park in that beautiful water you see. You can barely see it off in the distance, but there is a tiny little island a mile out that I've kayaked out to and had lunch. I also had this view for lunches many times back then from a bench that faces the Gulf from the park. My first encounter with "Bob" here too...LOL He's a Great Blue Heron. I call all the Great Blue Herons Bob now...hehehe

I haven't been there for quite some time now. I got out of the habit of visiting a few years ago. I'm not really sure why I stopped going. I'm grateful for the memories I have from when I first moved to Florida. This place still holds a special place in my heart these 4 years later.
