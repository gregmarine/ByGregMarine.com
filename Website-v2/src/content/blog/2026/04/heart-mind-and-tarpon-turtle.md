Last night was an enjoyable experience. We had dinner at Tarpon Turtle. I think this was my forth time eating there. Every time, it has been with my parents...hehehe We all planned to meet there at 5:30. I arrived at 5:25ish and got a text message that the rest of them were stuck in traffic. They didn't arrive until a little after 6, I believe. They had been in the car for over an hour...LOL I didn't run into any traffic, myself.

![First view of Lake Tarpon from Tarpon Turtle 🐢](https://res.cloudinary.com/dh6izdwyd/image/upload/v1777040030/IMG_8713_xk38zv.jpg "First view of Lake Tarpon from Tarpon Turtle 🐢")

I debated if I should go ahead and get a table without knowing when they'd arrive. The place wasn't too busy. So I went ahead and got us a table. They put us out by the water. It was a lovely evening to be by the lake instead of being in the dark dining room inside. As I sat there, I was visited by wildlife. Mostly the birds. But also some turtles.

I really didn't have a clue how long it would take them to arrive, but I also didn't want to fill up on an appetizer. So the waitress was kind enough to send me some chips and salsa to snack on while I waited. I was very grateful for her service last night. My family eventually arrived and I asked her if she could take our picture 🥰

![My family](https://res.cloudinary.com/dh6izdwyd/image/upload/v1777040031/IMG_8717_yhyjts.jpg "My family")

More wildlife showed up throughout our time together. There was a huge alligator just coasting on by. It was too far out for me to grab a video or photo of it...hehehe There was also some human activity on the lake which included one of those light wing aircraft that can takeoff and land on water. We found that to be funny since we saw airplanes on my mom's birthday last year too...LOL
