I have finally launched the new site I've been working on for a few weeks. I'm beyond excited for this season of the website. It has evolved quite a bit over the past few years. And this is the latest version of it live!

A few things of note are that I can now create new content by simply clicking a "New" button, writing the content, and then "Publish" it. As well as bringing my writings, art, and photos together on the site using the same easy of "New" -> "Publish" to quickly add those types of content as well.

I managed to migrate *all* of my previous blog posts. I haven't migrated anything else yet. But the blog is the main thing I've been maintaining since January of 2024. So it made the most sense to migrate first and get the new site online.

I can author the content in a web browser now. I can edit from my phone if I want. Or my MacBook. Or even most of my e-ink devices. This was something I simply could not do with the old way I setup back in the beginning.

I used Claude AI and Claude Code to bring this new site to reality. I needed to learn how it worked anyway. So this was a good project to give me a good idea of how those tools work. It was an amazing process. Especially for migrating the old content over. When I moved my site last year, I didn't bother migrating the 2024 posts because it was overwhelming. But with Claude, I was able to migrate all 408 posts from both previous sites to this one in a matter of minutes.
