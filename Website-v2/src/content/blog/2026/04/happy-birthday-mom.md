But then when I wrote, I forgot to mention how I liked that the next topic was on that same subject...hehehe I haven't read the devotional or the study passages yet. I simply noticed the title ❤️

Despite my lack of sleep, and the travel frustrations that caused the lack, I had a really good day yesterday. I managed to go through the workday. And then I spent the evening with my family. We had dinner at a place that was new to most of us. It had a sort of pirate theme. I went with the salmon. It was all pretty good and a nice atmosphere. My sister and mom did the usual *fighting* over the bill. I've learned to not get involved or even offer to pay. I find the opportune moments to pay for things and just let them hash it out otherwise. I was hoping to pay for lunch today. But they all decided they didn't want to come up here for Zen...LOL So I ate alone...HAHAHA I'll meet up with them for dinner later tonight. We'll see who is paying...LOL I'm guessing it will be my sister again. She won the battle last night 🤣

After dinner last night, though, we went back to my sister's place. We played Five Crowns; a really fun card game. I ended up winning 🤭 What's extra funny about that is the fact that the very first time I played it was exactly a year ago on their last visit down. I won that round too 🤣 I rarely win, though. It is a game of luck more than skill. It has become my parent's card game of choice for a little over a year now.

Today is Mom's birthday! This is the first one since her cancer diagnosis. So it's extra special. It reminds me of the first birthday she had after they discovered her other tumor just over 20 years ago. That one wasn't cancer. But it did almost kill her because of where it was. And she has been on a special treatment ever since that is keeping her alive. I'm not good with the technical names for such things. But she had *Elephant Man's Disease*. The last 20+ years have been special since she wasn't supposed to live. With her having cancer 6 months ago as well, I'm so grateful she's still here 🥹❤️
