I painted this 2x2 inch water color on canvas the same day I did "Sunset on Fuji." It's somewhat an expressive minimalism representation of where I was in September 2021. My potential was there. But I felt quite small. I was inspired by a small seedling I saw outside my window of the room I was renting at the time.
